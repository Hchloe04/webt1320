Disney A Christmas Carol

Disney a Christmas Carol features Jim Carrey as "Scrooge." As London awaits Christmas, Ebenezer Scrooge thinks it is humbug. Hating on his loyal worker and nephew for liking christmas and their views. Scrooge encounters the ghost of his late business partner, who warns him that he will be haunted by three spirits. Through the night the ghosts take scrooge on a journey through his past, present, and future. They do this in hopes of transforming his butterness for people and christmas.

The point of A Christmas Carol was for social commentary. Dickens the person who started all the carol stories, has a lifelong devotion to help the undeserved due to his own familys expirences with debtors prison, which forced him to drop out of school as a boy and work at a factory.

Number One All Time Christmas Movie

Today, It's a Wonderful Life is considered to be one of the greatest films of all time and among the best Christmas films. The year was 1946, and the world was much different than the one we know today. After the ravages of WWII, the gloom that had fallen over the world. Filmmakers were eager to share their part by making movies that are fool of hope and hapiness.

Frank Capra saw potential in other films and created the film "It's a Wonderful Life." The movie became a timeless classic. A tale of inspiration and many themes like hope, redemption, family, and selflessness. A perfect christmas story that was broadcasted, and soon was a staple Christmas film for families all over the world.
